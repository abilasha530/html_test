Gulab jamun

Ingredient

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • oil

Recipe

  1. Boil sugar and water to prepare syrup remove from fire. Add cardamom and keep aside to cool.
  2. In a mixing bowl put the flour, paneer, sooji, Nestl√© MILKMAID, baking powder and baking soda. Mix gently to make a soft dough. Do not knead too much.
  3. Divide the mixture into 30-35 portions and gently roll into round gulab jamuns. Fry in the oil at very low flame till golden brown in color.
  4. Keep putting the fried gulab jamuns in the prepared sugar syrup. Once all the gulab jamuns are in the sugar syrup bring it to a boil and remove from flame.
  5. Serve warm.

Barfi

Ingredient

  • Sugar
  • dry fruits
  • condensed milk

Recipe

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of ghee in a non-stick pan. Add 1 cup desiccated coconut and roast until light golden brown.
  2. Pour in 1 cup condensed milk and add 1/2 cup sugar. Mix well and cook over medium heat, stirring continuously, until the mixture thickens and leaves the sides of the pan (about 10-15 minutes).
  3. Optionally, add a pinch of cardamom powder for flavor. Mix it in.
  4. Turn off the heat and transfer the mixture to a greased tray or plate. Flatten it using a spatula.
  5. Allow the coconut barfi to cool for a few hours until set. Once set, cut into desired shapes. Enjoy your homemade coconut barfi!
